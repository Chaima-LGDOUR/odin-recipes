Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese Salad is a fresh Italian dish that highlights the simplicity and quality of its ingredients: ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. Originating from the island of Capri, this salad is a staple of Italian cuisine and is perfect for summer dining.

The combination of sweet tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil creates a colorful and delicious salad. It can be served as an appetizer, side dish, or light meal and is often seasoned with salt, pepper, and balsamic vinegar for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz (225 g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)

Steps

  1. Arrange: On a plate, alternate slices of tomato and mozzarella.
  2. Add Basil: Tuck fresh basil leaves between the layers.
  3. Season: Drizzle with olive oil, and add salt, pepper, and balsamic vinegar if desired.
  4. Serve: Enjoy fresh as a light meal or appetizer.
